Mona Lisa

The "Mona Lisa" is painted by Leonardo da Vinci in the early 16th century. It's a cornerstone of the Italian Renaissance. The artwork is famous for its enigmatic expression.

Marilyn Diptych

The "Marilyn Diptych" is created by Andy Warhol in 1962. It's a seminal piece in the Pop Art movement. The artwork examines the themes of fame and media through vivid colors and repetitive imagery.

Xiaolei Tian

The "Myth" series is by Tian Xiaolei. As a virtual digital artist, Tian uses 3D technology and digital media to explore themes of mythology and modern identity.
